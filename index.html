About me

I've been on both sides of the table

I'm an Engineering Manager at Mojang Studios — the Microsoft-owned studio behind Minecraft — and a Career Mentor with 15+ years in IT. I started as a test engineer before discovering that working with people and processes was just as rewarding as working with the product. Along the way I've hired engineers, led teams, and navigated career growth from both sides of the table.


Outside my day job, I co-founded LeadDev Stockholm, Sweden's engineering leadership community, and I facilitate #IamRemarkable workshops that help people talk about their accomplishments out loud. I know what it actually takes to grow in tech and make confident career decisions — beyond generic advice or online checklists. That same perspective shapes every LinkedIn session I run.
